Cheems Arena

Paused

Cheems Arena represents our boldest venture into the gaming world—a Free-to-Play (F2P) and Play-to-Earn (P2E) strategy Trading Card Game that transforms how players interact with their game assets. Unlike traditional trading card games, Cheems Arena allows players to own the assets they earn or purchase, fully backed by the robust Solana Blockchain. This integration not only supports the game’s NFTs and marketplace but also facilitates the use of our exclusive $CHEEMS tokens, enhancing both player engagement and asset liquidity.

Launched in Q4 2022 as a playable alpha, we invested over $150,000 into Cheems Arena, showcasing our commitment to its development and potential. However, due to an unforeseen bear market, player activity was not as high as anticipated, leading us to put the project on hold.

Current discussions are focused on a strategic relaunch, as we believe Cheems Arena was simply ahead of its time. With adjustments and renewed strategies, we are confident in the game's potential to make a significant impact. Stay tuned for the exciting return of Cheems Arena, poised to reshape the landscape of strategy trading card games.
